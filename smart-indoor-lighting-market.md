<p><p>The smart indoor lighting market includes various technologies such as fluorescent lamps, light-emitting diodes (LEDs), and high-intensity discharge (HID) lamps. Fluorescent lamps are energy-efficient, providing good illumination for large spaces but have shorter lifespans. LEDs are increasingly popular due to their longevity, low energy consumption, and versatility in smart applications. HID lamps are known for their intense brightness and efficiency, making them suitable for industrial and commercial use. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing energy efficiency and smart home integration.</p></p>
