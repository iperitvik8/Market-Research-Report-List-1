<p><p>The medical isolation eye masks market consists primarily of two types: tie-on and earloop masks. Tie-on masks feature long straps that can be tied behind the head, providing a secure fit and better coverage. They are often preferred in settings where adjustments are necessary. Earloop masks, on the other hand, use elastic loops that conveniently hook over the ears, offering ease of use and quick donning. Both types are essential for infection control in healthcare environments, ensuring protection for medical staff and patients.</p></p>
