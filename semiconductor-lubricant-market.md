<p><p>The semiconductor lubricant market encompasses various types, including PFPE (perfluoropolyether) and cyclopentane. PFPE lubricants are valued for their thermal stability, chemical resistance, and low volatility, making them ideal for high-performance applications in semiconductor manufacturing. Cyclopentane, on the other hand, is used primarily for its low viscosity and effective cooling properties, often in specific applications like vacuum pumps and equipment requiring efficient thermal management. Both types play critical roles in enhancing the performance and longevity of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
