<p><p>Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) systems can be categorized into three main market types based on lane exclusivity. </p><p>1. **Open Type Exclusive Lane**: Buses operate on lanes that are exclusively reserved for them but can be accessed by other vehicles at intersections.</p><p>2. **Semi-Open Exclusive Lane**: Buses have dedicated lanes that may be shared with other vehicles at certain times or segments, balancing efficiency and flexibility.</p><p>3. **Other Market**: This includes mixed-traffic systems or integrated public transport solutions that may not fit traditional classifications but still utilize BRT principles.</p></p>
