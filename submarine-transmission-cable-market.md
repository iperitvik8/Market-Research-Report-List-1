<p><ul><li>"DC Submarine Transmission Cable"</li><li>"AC Submarine Transmission Cable"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submarine transmission cable market comprises two main types: DC (Direct Current) and AC (Alternating Current) cables. DC submarine cables are primarily used for long-distance power transmission, enabling efficient energy transfer with minimal losses, making them ideal for connecting renewable energy sources to the grid. In contrast, AC submarine cables are commonly employed for shorter distances and are suited for integrating regional electricity networks. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing global interconnectivity and supporting the transition to renewable energy sources.</p></p>
