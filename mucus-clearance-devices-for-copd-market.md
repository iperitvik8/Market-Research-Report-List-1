<p><ul><li>HFCWO Devices</li><li>OPEP Devices</li><li>MCA Devices</li><li>IPV Devices</li><li>PEP Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mucus clearance devices for COPD aid in clearing airway secretions, enhancing lung function. HFCWO devices use high-frequency vibrations to loosen mucus. OPEP devices create positive expiratory pressure, helping to keep airways open and facilitate mucus removal. MCA devices employ mechanical techniques to support secretion clearance. IPV devices deliver vibratory aerosol therapy for effective clearance, while PEP devices utilize a resistor to maintain airway pressure and promote mucus expectoration. Together, these devices improve respiratory health in COPD patients.</p></p>
