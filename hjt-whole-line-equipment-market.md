<p><p>HJT Whole Line Equipment encompasses various technologies essential for fabricating high-efficiency solar cells. PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment is used for depositing thin films, enhancing cell performance. PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) equipment applies materials onto substrates through vaporization, improving conductivity and efficiency. Screen printing equipment is employed for applying metal contacts on solar cells, ensuring optimal electrical performance. Together, these tools facilitate the production of advanced solar cells, maximizing energy conversion in the photovoltaic industry.</p></p>
