<p><p>Equestrian insurance encompasses various types to protect horse owners and their assets. Major medical insurance covers extensive veterinary expenses due to illness or injury. Surgical insurance specifically addresses surgical costs. Full mortality insurance offers compensation if a horse dies, while limited mortality insurance covers only specific circumstances. Loss of use insurance compensates for decreased functionality that prevents the horse from performing as intended. Personal liability insurance protects against claims arising from injuries caused by the horse to others.</p></p>
