<p><p>The Electrosorption Modulator market is primarily categorized into two types: Absorptive Modulators and Refractive Modulators. Absorptive Modulators operate by altering the absorption characteristics of materials when subjected to an electric field, enabling enhanced performance in applications such as sensors and energy storage. In contrast, Refractive Modulators change the refractive index of a medium under the influence of an electric field, facilitating applications in telecommunications and imaging systems. Both types contribute to advancements in various technological fields.</p></p>
