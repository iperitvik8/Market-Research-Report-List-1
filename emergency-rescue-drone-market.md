<p><p>The emergency rescue drone market is segmented into two primary types: fixed-wing drones and rotor-wing drones. Fixed-wing drones are designed for long-range missions, offering higher endurance and speed, making them ideal for covering vast search areas quickly. In contrast, rotor-wing drones, or multirotors, provide greater maneuverability and can hover in place, making them suitable for precise rescues in complex environments. Each type serves unique operational needs in emergency response, enhancing efficiency and effectiveness in critical situations.</p></p>
