<p><p>One Way Vision Films are categorized based on apertures, which influence their application and visibility. "Aperture 1mm Below" films are designed for use on surfaces with limited visibility, ideal for indoor settings. "Aperture 1-1.5mm" films strike a balance, offering moderate visibility for both indoor and outdoor use, often found in retail environments. "Aperture 1.5mm Above" films enhance visibility and are suitable for outdoor advertising, providing clear graphics while allowing light transmission, making them a popular choice for storefronts and vehicle graphics.</p></p>
