<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time of Flight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D sensing array market primarily features two types: Structured Light and Time of Flight. Structured Light utilizes a projector to cast a series of light patterns onto an object, capturing the distortion to calculate depth. This method excels in controlled environments, providing high accuracy. Time of Flight, on the other hand, measures the time taken for light pulses to travel to an object and back, making it ideal for various lighting conditions. Both methods are essential for applications in robotics, autonomous vehicles, and augmented reality.</p></p>
