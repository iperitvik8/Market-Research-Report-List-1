The market includes several types: One-Handed valves allow quick operation with a single hand, facilitating easier control; Sideport Tubing valves offer additional access points for injections or drainage without interrupting the main flow; Straight Connectors provide direct connections between devices; and Y Connectors allow simultaneous connections to multiple devices, increasing versatility in fluid management. Each type enhances procedural efficiency and patient safety in clinical settings.</p></p>
