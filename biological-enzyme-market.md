<p><p>The biological enzyme market comprises various types that serve specific roles in biochemical processes. Acid reductases facilitate redox reactions, while transferases transfer functional groups between molecules. Hydrolytic enzymes break down substrates by hydrolysis, essential in digestion and metabolism. Isomerases catalyze the rearrangement of molecular structures, optimizing metabolic pathways. Synthetases are involved in synthesizing complex molecules, crucial for cellular function. Each enzyme type supports diverse applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food, driving innovation and efficiency.</p></p>
