<p><p>The embroidery machinery market is categorized primarily into single-head and multi-head machines. Single-head machines are designed for smaller-scale operations, allowing for detailed work on individual garments and flexibility in design. Conversely, multi-head machines are suitable for larger production demands, capable of embroidering multiple items simultaneously, which boosts efficiency and throughput. These types cater to different customer needs, with single-head machines favored by small businesses and hobbyists, while multi-head machines are preferred by larger firms aiming for high-volume production.</p></p>
