<p><strong>The Plastics Granulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beside-the-press Granulators</li><li>Central Granulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastics granulator market consists of various types, notably beside-the-press granulators and central granulators. Beside-the-press granulators are compact machines located next to injection molding machines, efficiently recycling sprues and misformed parts on-site. They enhance workflow by minimizing material waste. In contrast, central granulators are larger systems designed for processing significant volumes of plastic waste from multiple sources. They offer versatility in handling various materials, making them suitable for larger operations that demand higher production efficiency.</p></p>
