<p><p>The online fluoride analyzer market is segmented into two primary types: reagentless and reagent-based analyzers. Reagentless analyzers utilize advanced technology, such as ion-selective electrodes, to measure fluoride levels without the need for chemical reagents, offering lower maintenance and operational costs. In contrast, reagent-based analyzers require specific chemicals for accurate measurement, providing reliable results in various applications. Both types cater to industries like water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring compliance with health and safety standards.</p></p>
