<p><p>The digital veterinary thermometer market is primarily categorized into anal and ear types. Anal type thermometers are designed for rectal measurements, providing accurate internal body temperature readings, which are crucial for assessing the health of animals. Ear type thermometers, on the other hand, utilize infrared technology to measure temperature through the ear canal, offering quick and non-invasive readings. Both types are essential for veterinarians, enhancing efficiency in diagnostics and improving animal care through precise temperature monitoring.</p></p>
