<p><p>The Endocrine Disruption Screening Market is categorized into two primary types: in vitro and in vivo screening. In vitro screening involves laboratory tests using cell cultures or isolated tissues to assess the effects of substances on endocrine function, allowing for high-throughput analysis. In contrast, in vivo screening involves testing on live organisms to evaluate hormonal effects within a complex biological system, providing insights into the overall health impacts. Both approaches are essential for evaluating potential endocrine disruptors and ensuring safety.</p></p>
