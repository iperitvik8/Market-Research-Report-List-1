<p><p>The MRI contrast media market is primarily categorized into two types: injection type and oral type. Injection type contrast agents are administered intravenously, enhancing the visibility of blood vessels and tissues during imaging, often used for diagnosing various conditions. Each type serves distinct imaging purposes, allowing healthcare professionals to obtain clearer and more detailed MRI results, leading to improved diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
