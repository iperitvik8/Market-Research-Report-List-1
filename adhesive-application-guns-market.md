<p><p>Adhesive application guns are categorized into three main types: pneumatic, electric, and manual. Pneumatic guns use compressed air for operation, offering high speed and precision, ideal for high-volume applications. Electric guns operate on battery or mains power, providing consistent performance and ease of use for various projects. Manual guns rely on hand pressure for application, making them suitable for small tasks and providing greater control for intricate work. Each type caters to specific user needs and application scenarios.</p></p>
