<p><p>Oil-less scroll vacuum pumps are categorized into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided scroll pumps operate with a single scroll element, creating low to medium vacuum levels, ideal for applications where simplicity and maintenance efficiency are crucial. In contrast, double-sided scroll pumps utilize two scroll elements to generate higher vacuum levels more efficiently, often catering to industrial settings requiring robust performance. The choice between these types depends on the specific demands of the application, including vacuum level and operational efficiency.</p></p>
