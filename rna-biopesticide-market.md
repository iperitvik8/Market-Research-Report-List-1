<p><strong>The RNA Biopesticide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PIP</li><li>Non-PIP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RNA biopesticide market is divided into two main categories: PIP (Plant-Incorporated Protectants) and Non-PIP. PIP products involve genetically engineered plants that produce RNA molecules that target pests, offering a direct defense mechanism by utilizing the plant's own biological processes. In contrast, Non-PIP products consist of externally applied RNA biopesticides that can be sprayed on crops for pest management without altering the plant's genetic structure. Both types aim to enhance sustainable agriculture while minimizing chemical pesticide usage.</p></p>
