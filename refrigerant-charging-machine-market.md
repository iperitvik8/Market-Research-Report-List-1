<p><ul><li>Flammable Refrigerant Charging Machine</li><li>Non-Flammable Refrigerant Charging Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerant charging machine market is divided into two primary types: flammable and non-flammable refrigerant charging machines. Flammable refrigerant charging machines are designed for use with hydrocarbon refrigerants, which require specialized safety features due to their combustible nature. In contrast, non-flammable refrigerant charging machines are suitable for refrigerants such as Hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs) and other non-flammable alternatives. The choice between these types depends on regulatory standards, safety considerations, and the specific refrigerant being used in HVAC systems.</p></p>
