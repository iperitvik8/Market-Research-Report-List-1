<p><p>The end mills market is segmented into three main types: peripheral cutting edge, end cutting edge, and others. Peripheral cutting edge end mills are designed for side milling, enabling efficient lateral material removal. End cutting edge types focus on vertical cuts, allowing for precision in drilling and contouring. The 'others' category encompasses specialized tools like ball end mills and corner radius end mills, catering to specific applications in CNC machining and metalworking, enhancing versatility in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
