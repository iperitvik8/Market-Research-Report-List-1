<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The healthcare temperature-controlled packaging solution market is divided into two main types: active and passive systems. Active packaging involves dynamic temperature control mechanisms, such as refrigeration units or temperature-controlled logistics, ensuring that products remain within specific temperature ranges throughout transit. In contrast, passive packaging relies on insulating materials and design features to maintain temperature without external energy sources. Both types are essential for safeguarding sensitive pharmaceuticals and medical products during storage and distribution, ensuring product integrity and efficacy.</p></p>
