<p><p>The VR input device market is divided into two main types: traditional handles and motion-sensing handles. Traditional handles resemble conventional game controllers, offering buttons and analog sticks for precise input, typically used in seated gaming experiences. In contrast, motion-sensing handles utilize advanced sensors to detect user movements and gestures in three-dimensional space, allowing for more immersive interaction with virtual environments. Both types cater to different user preferences, enhancing the overall VR experience by providing tailored input methods.</p></p>
