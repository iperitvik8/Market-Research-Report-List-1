<p><p>The Scandium Oxide Sputtering Target Market comprises two main types: rotatable and non-rotating sputtering targets. Rotatable targets allow for continuous uniform deposition by rotating during the sputtering process, enhancing target utilization and efficiency. In contrast, non-rotating targets are fixed and rely on stationary sputtering, which may lead to an uneven deposition rate over time. Both types cater to various applications in electronics, optics, and thin-film coatings, offering unique advantages depending on the specific manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
