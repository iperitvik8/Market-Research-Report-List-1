<p><p>The mineral-oil-based lubricants market can be categorized into three main types: fluid, semi-fluid, and solid. Fluid lubricants, often in liquid form, provide excellent spreadability and are commonly used in engines and machinery. Semi-fluid lubricants maintain a gel-like consistency, offering enhanced adhesion and stability, ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Solid lubricants, available as powders or pastes, reduce friction in extreme conditions, commonly used in heavy machinery and high-load situations. Each type serves distinct applications based on lubrication needs.</p></p>
