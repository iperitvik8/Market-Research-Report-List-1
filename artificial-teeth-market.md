<p><p>The artificial teeth market encompasses three primary types: all-ceramic, metal-ceramic, and all-metal. All-ceramic teeth provide natural aesthetics and are favored for anterior restorations due to their translucency. Metal-ceramic restorations combine the strength of metal with the visual appeal of ceramic, making them versatile for both anterior and posterior applications. All-metal teeth, known for their durability and strength, are primarily used in areas requiring resilience, such as molars, albeit with less aesthetic appeal. Each type serves distinct patient needs.</p></p>
