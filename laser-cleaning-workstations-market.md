<p><strong>The Laser Cleaning Workstations Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"XY Direction"</li><li>"Rotary"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser cleaning workstations are categorized into "XY Direction" and "Rotary" types based on their operational movement. The XY Direction workstations utilize a two-axis system, allowing precise lateral movement across surfaces for effective cleaning of flat or complex geometries. In contrast, Rotary workstations are designed for cylindrical objects, enabling the laser to clean around curves and edges efficiently. Both types offer versatile applications across various industries, delivering a non-contact cleaning method that minimizes damage to the underlying material.</p></p>
