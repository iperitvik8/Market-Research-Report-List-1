<p><p>The Nitinol Stone Retrieval Basket market is categorized into two main types: tipless and tipped baskets. Tipless baskets feature a design without a specific tip, allowing for flexible navigation during retrieval procedures, which can be beneficial in challenging anatomical environments. Tipped baskets, on the other hand, have an angled tip that aids in grasping and retrieving stones more effectively. Both types are utilized in urological procedures, offering distinct advantages based on the complexity of the stone retrieval scenario encountered.</p></p>
