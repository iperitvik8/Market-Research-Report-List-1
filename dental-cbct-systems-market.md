<p><p>Dental CBCT systems are categorized into three market types based on the field of view they offer. </p><p>Small Field CBCT systems provide a limited view, ideal for single tooth imaging or localized analyses, making them suitable for endodontics and orthodontics. </p><p>Medium Field units have a broader perspective, accommodating multiple teeth or dental arches, useful for oral surgery or implant planning. </p><p>Large Field CBCT systems capture comprehensive 3D images of the entire maxillofacial region, essential for complex diagnostic procedures and treatment planning.</p></p>
