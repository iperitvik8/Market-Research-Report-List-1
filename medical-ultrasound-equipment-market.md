<p><p>The medical ultrasound equipment market can be categorized into two main types: compact type and table top type. Compact ultrasound systems are portable, lightweight, and designed for easy transport, making them suitable for point-of-care settings and remote areas. They provide essential imaging capabilities in a user-friendly format. In contrast, table top ultrasound units are larger and more stationary, offering advanced features and higher image quality, often utilized in hospitals and clinics for comprehensive diagnostics and specialized examinations.</p></p>
